About Myanmar Biodiversity Info System

The Myanmar (Burma) Biodiversity Information System is designed to act as an archival platform for historical, ecological, and modern avian literature.

Historical Sources Utilized

1909: Birds of Burma

By Major H.H. Harington. Written at the height of military survey expeditions, documenting primary forests that are now significantly degraded.

1953: The Birds of Burma

By Bertram E. Smythies. Legendary comprehensive ornithological catalogue, displaying extensive research on breeding behaviors across the Ayeyarwady Delta and Chin state hills.
