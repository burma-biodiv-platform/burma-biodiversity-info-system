Burma Biodiversity Information System

This is the data and system backbone of the Burma Biodiversity Platform. It stores, manages, and connects structured data on species, ecosystems, conservation status, and more.

  • Species Records Database
  • Data Entry Forms
  • APIs for Portal Integration
  • Language-Specific Metadata
  • Linked Datasets for Birds, Fishes, Plants, Mammals, Reptiles

Developed as part of the Burma Biodiversity Platform
